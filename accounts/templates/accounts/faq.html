Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EduBot system?

EduBot is an educational system designed to support children with learning difficulties. It combines humanoid robotics with a dedicated website for educators to select activities, monitor progress, and receive reports. The robot interacts with children using activities focused on motor skills, social integration, and personalized learning.

How does the EduBot system help children with learning difficulties?

EduBot uses a humanoid robot to engage children through interactive activities like counting games, color matching, and body part recognition. The system provides positive feedback and hints for incorrect answers, helping children to learn at their own pace in a supportive environment.

What types of activities can be selected for the children?

Educators can choose from various activities such as color matching, finger counting, shape recognition, and more. Each activity is designed to support cognitive and motor development while encouraging interaction and engagement through the humanoid robot or external hardware like buttons and sensors.
